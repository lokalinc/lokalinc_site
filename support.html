Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your LokaLinc account and associated data at any time.

To request account deletion:

  • Send an email to support@lokalinc.de
  • Use the subject: “Account Deletion Request”
  • Include your registered email address

What happens after your request:

  • Your account will be permanently deleted
  • Your personal data (such as name and email) will be removed
  • Your posts, messages, and other content may be deleted or anonymized

Processing time:
Requests are typically processed within 7 working days

Data retention:
Certain information may be retained if required for legal or security purposes
