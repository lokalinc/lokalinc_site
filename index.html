What you can do with LokaLinc

Temporary Housing

Find or offer short-term apartments (sublets) when relocating, travelling, or waiting for long-term housing in Germany.

Travel Support

Connect with trusted travelers who can carry small items (medicines) or documents between countries.

Community Discussions

Ask questions and share experiences about jobs, visas, healthcare, accommodation, and daily life in Germany.

Built for Expats

LokaLinc focuses on real challenges expats face — helping people connect and support each other.
